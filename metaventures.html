METAVENTURE

Your next leadership adventure.

MetaVenture is a leadership transformation and experiential adventure year for students aged 20–25. It’s a one-year process built around serving opportunities, leadership formation, and real experiences that shape authentic leaders.

ELIGIBILITY
  • • You must first complete your Metamorpho year as a student.
  • • Then you can apply to join MetaVenture

Where you can join

MetaVenture currently runs in these regions and partner campuses.
