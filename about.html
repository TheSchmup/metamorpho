Metamorpho is built around the belief of discovering authenticity

During a one-year process we navigate our students to take charge of their lives and to determine God’s purpose for their life. We have a firm belief in equipping students to have outstanding metacognitive skills and teach them how to utilize their unique gifts to contribute meaningfully to society. We wish to empower people between 18-23 years old to be the next generation of city changers and form part of a 25 year legacy.

Camp 02

Gung Ho

This is our team building camp. This Chinese word is defined as “working together”, which is exactly what you will be doing on this camp. We work with the basic understanding of teamwork as well as create reachable goals for your team. On Gung Ho you will realise how you form part of a team and also what gifts and skillset you can add.

Camp 03

Hiking Trail

We go on a hike of about 56 km between Sabie and Graskop. Over the course of this 4 day journey your character is put to the test and you are exposed the greatness of who God truly is. This is an exciting time where we focus on family and friendships sharing these moments together. Get your boots on, you do not want to miss this one.

What students experience

Meta is built around consistent rhythms. The details differ per campus, but the experience is designed to be structured, challenging, and deeply formative.

DISCIPLINE
Routines that build strength

Weekly structure that develops self-control, responsibility, and consistency.

FORMATION
Character + spiritual growth

Teaching, mentorship, and reflection that help discover and values.

PRACTICALITY
Skills for real life

Communication, teamwork, leadership, and practical competence through real responsibilities.

Outcomes we’re aiming for

  • • Greater maturity and ownership of life.
  • • Stronger habits and personal discipline.
  • • Clearer direction for study, work, and calling.
  • • Improved confidence, communication, and leadership.
  • • Healthier routines and stronger relationships.

Parents: what to expect

  • • Structured environment with mentorship and accountability.
  • • Clear communication pathways for questions and updates.
  • • A year that builds resilience, responsibility, and conviction.
